Duendes (2017-2018)

Lucrezia was a lead singer of Duendes, formed by 6 members: singer, saxophone, guitar, drums, double bass and piano player. They performed throughout London for events at the Thunderbirds in West Hampstead, at the King’s Eddie in Stratford, at the Strongroom Bar in Shoreditch, at the Alley Cats in Soho, at the Highway in Tufnell Park and in Highbury and Islington.
