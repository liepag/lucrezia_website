The Dawn of a Light (2019)
Lucrezia Vittoria · The Dawn of A Light

"The Dawn of Light" is an abstract piece conceived and directed by Lucrezia Vittoria Chelini. This piece was created to analyze the change that can affect a human's perception of existense and reality within a situation of stillness. The interest in this matter was born out of the observation, that as a species, we humans tend to focus on the past or future and have difficulty focussing on the actual 'process' that leads us to change for the better or worse.


To represent this she uses the narrative of a creature that has lived its entire life in a reality of stilness, fear and judgement. In a moment of despair, the creature makes the decision to break through this perceived reality and battles to take a new path in life. This transition is guided by the creature's conscience that strives to connect him to his deepest thoughts and diseres that evaded him since birth. The creature's mind and conscience work together and push the creature towards a new found discovery that will change his reality forever. The piece concludes with the creature's first steps into the new world. We never find out if its new version of himself is postive or negative. All we know that the change has hapenned.


To tell this story Lucrezia has composed an eclectic music soundscape accompanied by poetry, movement and singing. Each art form has been used and assorted together to reach and connect with the audience.
